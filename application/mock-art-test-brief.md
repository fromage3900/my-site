# Mock Infold-Style Art Test Brief

## Goal
Create a small stylized fantasy environment slice that proves mood, material taste, and production discipline.

## Target
A quiet magical path, courtyard, grove, or boutique-like outdoor landmark with one clear player route and one collectible focal object.

## Scope
- One hero camera
- One alternate detail camera
- Three material families
- One procedural dressing pass
- One breakdown page
- One short production note

## Day 1
- Define the fantasy hook in one sentence
- Build a reference board
- Block out route, silhouette, focal point, and camera
- Choose palette and lighting mood

## Day 2
- Build or adapt master material instances
- Add surface motifs: blossom, moss, celestial accent, gilded trim, soft cloth, paper, or stone
- Add scatter pass with exclusion zones and artist controls
- Capture before-after images

## Day 3
- Polish lighting and composition
- Render final hero and detail plates
- Export shader/material callouts
- Write constraints, timing, optimization notes, and next improvements

## Scoring
- 60 percent visual charm and composition
- 25 percent technical repeatability
- 15 percent communication polish
