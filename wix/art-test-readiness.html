Art Test Shape

Small scene, high taste, clear systems.

The best test target is a vertical slice: one readable destination, three material families, one procedural dressing pass, and one polished hero frame.

Day 01

Brief, blockout, and mood.

Define fantasy hook, reference board, camera path, palette, composition hierarchy, and production scope.

Day 02

Materials and dressing.

Build stylized master instances, blossom or moss dressing, trimsheet accents, and player-readable path support.

Day 03

Render, optimize, explain.

Ship hero frame, detail plates, shader notes, stats, and a concise review page.

Scoring Rubric

What to optimize for.

This keeps new work aimed at the role instead of drifting into generic portfolio content.

60%

Visual charm and composition.

Immediate fantasy appeal, graceful color, readable route, silhouette clarity, and emotional specificity.

25%

Technical repeatability.

Material architecture, procedural dressing, clean handoff, reusable controls, and performance awareness.

15%

Communication polish.

Concise labels, process clarity, before-after proof, and no buried best work.
