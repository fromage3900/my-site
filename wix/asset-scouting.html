Promotion Rules

Only the best work gets front-page weight.

This prevents the portfolio from becoming an archive. Recruiters should see a confident sequence, not a folder dump.

Keep

Fantasy appeal in five seconds.

Promote if the image has immediate mood, clean silhouette, and a clear reason to exist on an Infold-facing page.

Refine

Good idea, not final yet.

Move into case-study support or paintover queue when the idea is strong but polish is uneven.

Archive

Does not serve the role.

Keep off the live site if it is generic, noisy, too old, low resolution, or disconnected from the hiring narrative.
